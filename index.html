Bachelor in Computer Technology

The BCT is a four-year degree program thath focuses on the practical aspects of the science of computing. The Program develops core skills in multimedia technology, web mobile dvelopment and networks system administration. Furthermore, it prepares learners with supervisory and management skills. The curriculum is derived from BS Industrial Technology major in Computer Technology with CHED Certificate of Program Compliance (COPC) No. 31, series of 2021.

Program Objectives:

The Program prepares learners with the practical skills in multimedia technology,web/mobile development, and supervisorial skills to manage an organization in these areas of competencies, engage in a startup business or pursue a teaching career.

Specific Objectives:

The learners should be able to:
  1. Demonstrate understanding of the important of being an ethical computing professional when making work-related decisions.
  2. Develop products or process by applying their knowledge in computer or network system, and developmentor productivity tools.
  3. Participate as an active member of a development/service team with leadership roles when appropriate.
  4. Apply communication skills effectively to promote ideas, goals, process or products.
  5. Make positive contributions to the community with the skills and abilities learned in the program.
  6. Pursue lifelong learning for personal and professional growth through varied experiental and feiled-based opportunities.
